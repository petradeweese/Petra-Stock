Privacy Policy

Effective: {{ current_year() }}

Petra Stock collects only the information required to deliver pattern alerts and maintain SMS compliance. This includes the phone numbers you verify, consent records (timestamp, IP, user agent, method), and basic messaging metadata such as delivery status and opt-out events.

How we use your information

  • Send the SMS and email alerts you explicitly requested.
  • Log consent details so we can honor STOP/START/HELP keywords.
  • Analyze aggregate usage trends to improve alert quality.

Message frequency varies by market activity and your settings, typically up to 10 msgs/day. Msg & data rates may apply. Alerts are informational only and not financial advice.

Opt-out and deletion

Reply STOP to any Petra Stock SMS to opt out immediately. Reply HELP for assistance. You may email privacy@petrastock.com to request deletion of consent logs where permitted by law.

Data retention

We retain consent records for audit purposes while you are subscribed and as required to demonstrate compliance. Delivery metadata is kept for a limited period to troubleshoot issues and satisfy carrier requests.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@petrastock.com or support@petrastock.com.

Petra Stock will update this page if practices change and notify subscribers as required.
